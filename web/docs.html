Quick start

Three fast moves.

  • Invite the bot into your server.
  • Run /maxplay to start the first round.
  • Use the action buttons to keep the flow moving without retyping commands.

Game flow

What a normal round looks like.

Step 1

Invite and sync

Add the bot, confirm the slash commands show up, and test in one room first.

Step 2

Run /maxplay

Launch a random, truth, or dare round without extra setup friction.

Step 3

Stay in motion

Pick Truth, Take Dare, and Mix It keep the pace up once the chat warms up.

Step 4

Adjust and monitor

Use the rest of the deck to tune the server, schedule drops, and verify runtime health.

Slash commands

The current command family.

/maxplay

Launch the main game flow with a random, truth, or dare style round.

Action buttons

What people see after the first round starts.

Pick Truth

Stay in confession mode.

Useful when the room wants more talk, more reactions, and less performance pressure.

Take Dare

Push the energy up.

Shift the round into faster, more performative chaos when the room is ready for it.

Mix It

Let the bot decide.

Good when the group wants variety and you do not want to over-manage each round.
