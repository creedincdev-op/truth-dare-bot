Privacy policy

Current privacy notice.

Effective date: March 13, 2026.

Scope

Website and bot usage

This notice covers the public website, support routes, and normal use of the Truth OR Dare bot experience.

Information we process

The public website is designed to expose product information, runtime health, and support links. Hosting providers may process standard request data such as IP address, browser data, and access logs to deliver the service.

When you use the bot inside Discord, Discord may provide server, channel, interaction, and user identifiers needed for command execution, anti-repeat behavior, and runtime operation.

How information is used

Operational data may be used to run the bot, respond to commands, prevent rapid prompt repetition, monitor uptime, and troubleshoot failures.

Third-party services

The product may rely on Discord, hosting providers, and optional AI providers when configured. Their own terms and privacy practices apply to the parts of the experience they provide.

Retention

Runtime and log data may be retained only as long as reasonably needed for bot operation, diagnostics, abuse prevention, or service improvement.

Contact route

For current support or privacy questions, use the support server linked on the support page.
